Warranty Policy

Atx-condo, we are committed to providing you with high-quality products and exceptional service. Our warranty policy ensures peace of mind and protection for your purchase. Below are the details of our warranty coverage:

Warranty Coverage

Our products are covered under a limited warranty against manufacturing defects, starting from the date of purchase. This warranty includes:

  • Faulty workmanship
  • Defective materials
  • Malfunctioning components under normal use

Warranty Period

  • Electronics: 1 Year from the date of purchase
  • Accessories: 6 Months from the date of purchase
  • Batteries: 6 Months from the date of purchase

Note: Warranty periods may vary for specific products. Please refer to the product packaging or invoice for details.
Whatâ€™s Not Covered

Our warranty does not cover the following:

  • Physical damage caused by misuse, accidents, or unauthorized repairs
  • Damage caused by water, fire, or other external factors
  • Normal wear and tear, such as fading, scratches, or cosmetic issues
  • Issues arising from the use of incompatible accessories or third-party products

How to Claim Your Warranty

  1. Contact Us: Reach out to our support team via email, phone, or at a physical store location.
  2. Provide Proof of Purchase: Present your receipt, invoice, or warranty card for verification.
  3. Inspection: Our team will inspect the product to determine eligibility under the warranty.
  4. Resolution: Eligible products will be repaired, replaced, or refunded at our discretion.

Warranty Claim Process Timeline

Once a claim is filed, we aim to process and resolve it within 7-14 business days. If additional time is needed, you will be informed promptly.

Additional Notes

  • Repairs and replacements are subject to product availability.
  • Warranty claims are only valid for the original purchaser and are non-transferable.
